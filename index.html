About Me

Full-stack software developer with experience building and maintaining applications across cybersecurity, education, and financial administration sectors. My journey started in Florida, where living and studying through middle school and high school gave me a strong bilingual foundation, cultural adaptability, and an early international perspective that still shapes how I communicate and work today.

While attending West Orange High School, I was part of the AFJROTC program, an experience that helped develop my discipline, leadership mindset, and sense of responsibility. It was also there that I became more serious about technology through the CyberPatriot program, which introduced me to cybersecurity and IT in a practical way, and later through advanced coursework in high school such as AP Computer Science Principles. Those experiences played an important role in moving me toward Computer Science as a long-term path.

After moving to Brazil, I earned my Computer Science degree from the University of Fortaleza (UNIFOR) and built my career in full-stack software development, contributing to highly used enterprise systems with real operational impact. Throughout my professional experience, I have worked on platforms relied on by large numbers of users, and I currently contribute to an international enterprise project with presence across North America, South America, Europe, and Asia. Alongside my professional work, I continue to deepen my interest in computer vision and artificial intelligence through academic and personal projects.
